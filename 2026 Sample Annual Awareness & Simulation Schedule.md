### **Recurring Monthly Activities**
| Activity | Frequency | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Security Newsletter** | Monthly | Enterprise-wide newsletter covering threats, fraud trends, and key behaviors. |
| **Awareness Webinar** | Twice per month | Live sessions focused on human-risk themes, social engineering, and emerging threats. |
| **Technology Topic Webinar** | Monthly | Deep dives for technical teams (cloud security, IAM, secure coding, etc.). |
| **Phishing Simulations** | Monthly | Varying complexity, targeted by role, business unit, and risk profile. |
| **Communications Releases** | Monthly | Messaging aligned to all scheduled activities and threat conditions. |

### **Quarterly Activities**
| Activity | Frequency | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Work Area Security Desk Sweeps** | Quarterly | Physical security and clean desk checks for designated sites. |
| **Quarterly Risk Review** | Quarterly | Review of behavioral indicators, simulation data, and targeted training needs. |

### **Annual Activities**
| Activity | Frequency | Description |
|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Annual Required Risk Training** | Once per year | Mandatory enterprise-wide training aligned to regulatory expectations. |
| **Privileged Access Training** | Once per year | Required for high-risk roles (admins, developers, finance, executives). |
| **Targeted Training** | Ad hoc | Triggered by behavioral analytics, simulation failures, or incident trends. |
