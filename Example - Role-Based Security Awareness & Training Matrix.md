# 🧩 Role-Based Security Awareness & Training Matrix

### *Risk-Based Training Alignment — Financial Institution Example*

This example demonstrates how security awareness and learning content can be aligned to employee risk profiles, operational responsibilities, and threat exposure within a financial institution environment.

| Employee Group | Primary Risk Areas | Training Focus | Delivery Frequency |
|---|---|---|---|
| All Employees | Phishing, password hygiene, social engineering | Core cybersecurity awareness and secure behaviors | Annual + Monthly Reinforcement |
| Executives & Leadership | BEC, deepfakes, targeted phishing, crisis communications | Executive cyber preparedness and fraud awareness | Quarterly |
| Help Desk & Support Teams | Social engineering, MFA bypass, identity verification fraud | Identity validation and escalation procedures | Monthly |
| Finance & Payroll Teams | Wire fraud, invoice fraud, payment diversion | Fraud detection and transaction verification | Monthly |
| Technology & Engineering Teams | Secure coding, privileged access misuse, cloud risk | Technical security awareness and operational security | Quarterly |
| Privileged Users & Administrators | Credential theft, PAM misuse, insider risk | Elevated access security responsibilities | Quarterly |
| Customer-Facing Teams | Impersonation scams, account takeover attempts | Fraud prevention and customer protection awareness | Quarterly |
| Remote Workforce | Physical security, remote access security, device protection | Secure remote work practices | Semi-Annual |
| Third-Party Contractors | Data handling, acceptable use, access security | Third-party security expectations and policy compliance | Onboarding + Annual |
| New Hires | Security culture, acceptable use, phishing awareness | Foundational enterprise security onboarding | During Onboarding |

### 🎯 Program Objectives

- Deliver risk-based and role-appropriate awareness education
- Reduce human-enabled cyber and fraud risk
- Reinforce operational and regulatory expectations
- Improve phishing and social engineering resilience
- Support measurable behavioral change across the enterprise
- Align awareness efforts to NIST CSF and CRI framework guidance
