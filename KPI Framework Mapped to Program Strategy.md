## **Comprehensive KPI Framework Aligned to Program Strategy**

This KPI framework provides measurable indicators for every component of the Security Awareness & Human Risk Management Program. KPIs are aligned to program strategy, mapped to CRI/NIST controls, and designed to demonstrate quantifiable human‑risk reduction within a financial institution.

---

# **1. Threat-Informed Awareness KPIs**
Measures how effectively the program adapts to real-world threats and CTI inputs.

| KPI | Description | Target | Strategic Alignment | CRI/NIST Mapping |
|-----|-------------|---------|---------------------|------------------|
| **CTI-to-Campaign Adoption Rate** | % of monthly campaigns derived from CTI | >80% | Threat-informed operations | AT‑2, AT‑3 |
| **Time-to-Integrate New Threats** | Days from CTI alert → awareness action | <10 days | Adaptive awareness | IR‑4, PM‑12 |
| **CTI-Relevant Reporting Rate** | % of reported emails matching active threats | >40% | Reporting behavior | AU‑6, IR‑6 |
| **CTI-Driven Simulation Accuracy** | % of simulations modeled on real TTPs | >90% | Threat realism | AT‑3 |
