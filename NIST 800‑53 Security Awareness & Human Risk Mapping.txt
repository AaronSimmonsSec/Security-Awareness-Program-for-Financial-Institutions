# 🛡️ CRI / NIST CSF / NIST 800‑53 Security Awareness & Human Risk Mapping
### Financial Services Security Awareness & Human Risk Management Framework

This mapping aligns CRI Profile domains with NIST CSF, NIST 800‑53, and enterprise Security Awareness capabilities for financial institutions. Focus areas include Security Awareness, Human Risk Management, Fraud Readiness, Identity Awareness, Incident Readiness, and Security Culture.

---

## 1. High‑Level Framework Alignment

| CRI Domain | NIST CSF Alignment | NIST 800‑53 Families | Security Awareness Relevance |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------------------|------------------------------|
| Cyber Risk Governance | Govern (GV) | PM, PL, CA | Executive oversight, awareness governance |
| Identity & Access Management | Protect (PR.AA) | AC, IA | MFA, credential hygiene, privileged access awareness |
| Cybersecurity Awareness & Training | Protect (PR.AT) | AT | Enterprise awareness & role-based training |
| Threat & Vulnerability Management | Detect (DE) | RA, SI | Threat-informed campaigns |
| Incident Response | Respond (RS) | IR | Escalation awareness & incident readiness |
| Data Protection | Protect (PR.DS) | MP, SC | Data handling awareness |
| Fraud Prevention | Protect / Detect | AC, AU, IR | Social engineering & fraud readiness |
| Operational Resilience | Recover / Respond | CP, IR | Crisis readiness & resilience awareness |
| Third-Party Risk | Identify (ID.SC) | SR | Vendor awareness & external risk education |
