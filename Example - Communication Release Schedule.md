# 📅 Cybersecurity Awareness & Communications Release Schedule

> **Enterprise Security Awareness Program**  
> Financial Institution — Communication & Engagement Cadence

---

# 🎯 Program Objectives

The cybersecurity communications release schedule is designed to:
- Maintain consistent employee engagement
- Reinforce secure behaviors
- Improve awareness of emerging threats
- Support policy and compliance initiatives
- Increase participation in awareness events and training
- Strengthen organizational security culture

---

# 🗓️ Standard Communication Cadence

| Communication Type | Audience | Frequency | Release Timing | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly Security Newsletter | All Employees | Monthly | Business Day 1 of Each Month | Security Awareness Team |
| Event Reminder Email | Registered Participants | Per Event | 1 Business Day Prior to Event | Security Awareness Team |
| Event Thank You Email | Event Participants | Per Event | 1 Business Day After Event | Security Awareness Team |
| Cyber Alert Notifications | Impacted Employees / Enterprise | Adhoc | As Threat Intelligence Warrants | Cyber Threat Intelligence / Security Awareness |
| Intranet Event Promotion | All Employees | Monthly | One Featured Event Per Month | Security Awareness Team |
| Cyber Risk Posture Change Communications | Impacted Employee Groups | Adhoc | Upon Policy / Standard Change Approval | Cybersecurity Governance |
| Cybersecurity Awareness Month Campaign | All Employees | Annual | October | Security Awareness Team |
| Annual Training Launch Communication | All Employees + Managers | Annual | Upon Annual Training Release | Security Awareness Team |
| Annual Training Reinforcement Reminder | Employees with Incomplete Training | Annual | 30 Days Prior to Training Due Date | Security Awareness Team / Managers |

---

# 📧 Communication Types & Purpose

---

## 📰 Monthly Security Newsletter

### Purpose
Provide consistent cybersecurity awareness education and organizational updates.

### Typical Content
- Emerging threats
- Social engineering awareness
- Security tips
- Upcoming events
- Security program highlights
- Industry-relevant cyber risks

### Release Standard
- Distributed on the **first business day of each month**

---

## ⏰ Event Reminder Emails

### Purpose
Increase attendance and participation in awareness sessions and webinars.

### Typical Content
- Event start time
- Meeting links
- Agenda/topics
- Calendar reminder
- Speaker information

### Release Standard
- Sent **1 business day prior** to scheduled event

---

## ✅ Event Follow-Up / Thank You Emails

### Purpose
Reinforce learning and provide access to materials.

### Typical Content
- Thank you message
- Presentation materials
- Session recording
- Additional resources
- Feedback survey

### Release Standard
- Sent **1 business day after** scheduled event

---

## 🚨 Cyber Alert Notifications

### Purpose
Rapid awareness communication for active or emerging threats.

### Trigger Sources
- Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI)
- Security Operations Center (SOC)
- Fraud Intelligence
- Vendor advisories
- Government / industry alerts

### Example Topics
- Active phishing campaigns
- MFA fatigue attacks
- Deepfake scams
- Credential theft campaigns
- Business email compromise trends

### Release Standard
- **Adhoc based on threat activity**

---

## 🌐 Intranet Event Promotion

### Purpose
Drive awareness and participation through internal communications channels.

### Typical Content
- Featured monthly awareness event
- Security awareness spotlight
- Awareness month promotions
- Webinar banners

### Release Standard
- One promoted event featured each month

---

## 📋 Cyber Risk Posture Change Communications

### Purpose
Notify impacted employee groups of policy, standard, or procedural updates.

### Example Topics
- MFA requirement changes
- Password policy updates
- Data handling requirements
- Acceptable use changes
- Remote work security expectations

### Release Standard
- Distributed upon approved policy or control changes

### Target Audience
- Only impacted employee groups or business units

---

# 🎃 Cybersecurity Awareness Month (October)

### Annual Campaign Objectives
- Increase enterprise-wide engagement
- Reinforce human risk reduction behaviors
- Promote security culture initiatives

### Typical Activities
- Weekly awareness themes
- Phishing simulations
- Security games/challenges
- Executive messaging
- Giveaways and recognition
- Awareness webinars

### Release Standard
- Campaign runs throughout October

---

# 🎓 Annual Security Training Communications

## Initial Training Launch

### Purpose
Announce release of annual required security awareness training.

### Audience
- All employees
- People managers
- Contractors (where applicable)

### Typical Content
- Completion expectations
- Due dates
- Access instructions
- Manager accountability expectations

### Release Standard
- Sent once annually upon training launch

---

## 📣 Annual Training Reinforcement Campaign

### Purpose
Drive completion rates prior to training close date.

### Target Audience
- Employees with incomplete training
- Managers with outstanding direct reports

### Typical Content
- Remaining completion deadlines
- Escalation reminders
- Compliance expectations

### Release Standard
- Sent beginning **30 days prior to training deadline**

# 🛡️ Governance & Oversight

The communication release schedule should align with:
- Enterprise security strategy
- Regulatory expectations
- Human risk management objectives
- Audit and compliance requirements
- Cyber threat intelligence operations

Periodic reviews should be conducted to ensure:
- Messaging effectiveness
- Communication consistency
- Audience targeting accuracy
- Employee engagement improvement
